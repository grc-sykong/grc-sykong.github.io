Introduction

The Revival

The Revival is a celebration of R&B’s enduring legacy and its contemporary resurgence. Within these pages, we invite you to explore the soulful, groovy, and melodic landscapes that define R&B. Let us bring you closer to the stories, voices, and rhythms that have shaped R&B's past and are currently charting its future.
